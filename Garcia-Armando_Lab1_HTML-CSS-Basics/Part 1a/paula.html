Paula Scher

Company: Pentagram
Position: Partner, Graphic Designer, Painter, Educator

About: From pentagram.com/about/paula-scher, For four decades Paula Scher has been at the forefront of graphic design. Described as the master conjurer of the instantly familiar, Scher straddles the line between pop culture and fine art in her work. Iconic, smart, and accessible, her images have entered into the American vernacular. Paula Scher has taught me about technique, and also how past design can still function in today's society. Her approach to the design process is one-of-a-kind. I'm inspired by her dedication to disrupt the everyday with bright, bold, graphic emphasis.
